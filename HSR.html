Honkai: Star Rail

Description

Honkai: Star Rail is a turn-based space fantasy RPG where players embark on an interstellar journey aboard the Astral Express. They explore diverse worlds, meet unique characters, and uncover the mysteries of the Stellaron.

Story

Players take on the role of the Trailblazer, who awakens with a Stellaron inside their body. They join the Astral Express crew to travel across the cosmos, seeking answers about the Stellaron and the threats it poses. The game explores themes of destiny, choice, and the vastness of the universe.
