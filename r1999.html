Reverse: 1999

Description

Reverse: 1999 is a time-traveling strategic RPG featuring stylish 20th-century aesthetics. Players control Arcanists, individuals with magical abilities, as they navigate a mysterious temporal storm.

Story

The story centers around a phenomenon called the "Storm," which reverses time at the end of each year. Players take on the role of Vertin, the Timekeeper, who is immune to the Storm's effects. Vertin must travel through different eras, recruit Arcanists, and uncover the secrets behind the Storm to prevent the complete erasure of history.
