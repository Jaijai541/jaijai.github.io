Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

Description

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a fast-paced 5v5 multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game for mobile devices. Players choose from a large roster of heroes with unique abilities and battle to destroy the enemy's base.

Story

Heroes battling across the land of dawn, and the battles between good and evil. Each hero has their own backstory, and relationships with other heroes.
